Terraform commands
1. terrafrom init
2. terrafrom plan
3. terraform apply
4. terraform show (gives full output of changes we made)
5. terraform workspace new <space like dev,stage, prod...>( mention space <>)
6. terraform workspace select <space> (under space mention dev, prod, depends on the stages we have)
7.tree(gives you structure)
